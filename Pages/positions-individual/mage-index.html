MID

The magician is associated with the middle lane or "MID", is the most difficult laning role


Occupy the mid lane. As Mid, you are the guy who looks for, and usually finishes, teamfights in the later stages of the game.

Your lane is the most contested lane in the game, as both teams need to pass through it constantly, so large-scale conflicts occur much more easily in the middle. The middle is arguably the most difficult of the "lane" roles, as you have to pay attention to the map to know when you think you can initiate a team fight in your team's favor. Also, since your damage is usually in the form of abilities, it is also usually a more mechanically demanding role, depending on the god you choose. Lastly, since your lane has a lot of traffic, you should also be the guy who alerts when you see enemies crossing your lane, so your team can react or counter accordingly.


Ideal if you like team fighting, with a more active gameplay when it comes to juggling your aggressiveness and keeping an eye on all enemies for opportune targets.
