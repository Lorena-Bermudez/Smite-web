WARRIOR

SOLO/TOP

The warrior is associated with the TOP/SOLO lane, it is the most difficult laning role.


Solo is the role that operates uniquely to the other lanes, it is almost totally divorced from the rest of the game until the teamfighting phase. It occupies the lane whose towers are slightly closer together.

Your job is to duel the enemy Solo in terms of accumulating Gold and EXP, to gain a level advantage over him and his team in general for the strategy game, usually by playing the role of a secondary tank or hybrid God. The benefit of gaining an advantage over your mirror is so important to Solo, that your item build will rely almost entirely on countering your mirror, match by match (meaning Solo focuses more on item store knowledge). While it's not the most action-packed role, it's straightforward, and you're almost entirely responsible for your own play/performance.


Perfect if you like to know what you are going to do in a given game, prefer individual dueling to team fighting.
