GUARDIAN

SUPPORT

The guardian is associated with SUPPORT, it is a role that focuses on helping the team (lives for the team and dies for the team).


Initially with the ADC, he occupies the Duo lane on the Golden Fury side (whose towers are slightly further apart) before controlling the entire area of the map from the ADC to the friendly half.

They generally focus on Crowd Control to set up kills for their allies and build en masse (if you're new to MOBAs, support doesn't equate to always healing teammates, it's just a potential secondary factor). Support is the least played, least appreciated and most long-term impactful role in the game. A role where you are expected to die and where you need to stay alive. Where you have to read minds and make your own decisions. A thankless job for which you'll have to get used to being criticized, because even if you do well, it's often hard to tell.


If you like being extremely hard to kill, being the lynchpin of a team fight, pulling the enemy out of their plans to kill your teammates and don't mind too much letting kills go by... or, in general, you like being the good guy on the team, support is your role.
