JUNGLER

The assassin is associated with JUNGLERs, is a complex role that occupies the jungle, and is expected to help all lanes accordingly.


Assassins tend to have good pursuit, mobility, good camp clearance and a great burst in the early stages of the game.

You are the glue that holds the team together and will respond to everything in every game. A good Jungler can carry an entire team, and barely be seen while doing so, but that has the disadvantage that you definitely need to know what you're doing and control a lot of the game and the timing.


Undoubtedly, Jungler is the most difficult role in the game, and has a high level of skill that reflects this.
