HUNTER

ADC

The Hunter or Carry is associated with the ADC (Attack Damage Carry), it is a laning role that focuses on damage.


He focuses on farming his lane, specializes in damage and relies on his team to protect him.

This is the role that occupies the Duo lane on the Gold-Fury side; whose towers are slightly further apart from each other. It tends to have a focus on Basic Attack Damage (ideally of the Physical type). The reason for this is simple - something has to be able to quickly destroy Structures / kill Jungle Bosses, and kill the Titan. Basic Physical + Ranged Attacks are the best for this. Without a God that can quickly destroy a Structure, a team will have a much harder time finishing a game. Your goal is to do as much damage as possible and get to the point where you are doing that damage as quickly as possible.


It is a role made for those who focus on kills.
