SLASH

INFO

Slash is a fun 5v5 mode highlighting the Egyptian and Maya
Pantheons in SMITE. The Slash map features two lanes
surrounded by a jungle area. This game also includes unique
objectives like Apophis, a strong jungle camp, and Juggernauts,
Apophis’s chosen warriors to help . A standard Slash game will
take about 20 minutes, and you’ll earn the standard match
rewards for this mode.

MAP OBJECTIVE

Destroy opposing towers, phoenixes, and defeat the enemy
Titan with the help of the unique jungle camps on the map

PLAYERS

Solo up to a party of 5.

PVP REWARDS

You will earn normal match rewards

GAME DURATION

20 minuts
