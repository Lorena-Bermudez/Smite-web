INFO

Joust mode features a single-lane map and a side jungle. Your
base, defended by one Phoenix and a Titan, sits on one end.
Your enemy's base is on the other. Average game time is
around 15 minutes.

MAP OBJECTIVE

Destroy the opposing tower, Phoenix and Titan.
Other map objectives are the neutral buff camps and the Bull
Demon King.

PLAYERS

Solo up to a party of 3.

PVP REWARDS

You will earn normal match rewards

GAME DURATION

15 minuts
